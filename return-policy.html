All refund requests must be submitted within fourteen (14) days of the purchase date or renewal date.

REFUNDS

After receiving your refund request, we will process it within ten (10) business days. We will notify you by email when your refund has been processed.

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • The refund request is submitted more than 14 days after the purchase or renewal date.
